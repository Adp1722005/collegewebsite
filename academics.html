DEPARTMENT OF COMPUTER SCIENCES

The front line department of COMPUTER SCIENCE was established in 1996 with a goal to produce hardcore professionals to meet global challenges. The department is a large one and thriving with a reputation for work in many branches of the discipline. The department has dedicated, experienced and well qualified regular faculty, excellent Computer and Electronics laboratories with state-of-art-facilities.

BACHELOR OF COMPUTER APPLICATIONSB.C.A. : 3 Years (6 Semesters) Higher Secondary (10 +2) Certificate examination of M.P Board or its equivalent examination with Mathematics
BACHELOR OF SCIENCE (ELECTRONICS)B.Sc. (C.S.) : 3 Years (6 Semesters) Higher Secondary (10 +2) Certificate examination of M.P Board or its equivalent examination with Science Subjects.
POST GRADUATE DIPLOMA IN COMPUTER APPLICATIONSP.G.D.C.A. : 1 Year (2 Semester) A Bachelor’s Degree of any discipline from a recognized University with at least 50% Marks in Aggregate. (SC / ST / OBC 45 % marks in aggregate)
BACHELOR OF SCIENCE (COMPUTER SCIENCE)B.Sc. (C.S.) : 3 Years (6 Semesters) Higher Secondary (10 +2) Certificate examination of M.P Board or its equivalent examination with Mathematics.

DEPARTMENT OF BIO SCIENCES

The forefront department of BIOSCIENCES was established in 2002 to produce hardcore professionals to meet global challenges. The department provides an exciting and supportive environment for graduates and post-graduates students. In addition, the department has close links with the department of Chemistry and Computer Science. There is an exciting programme of external seminars by renowned speakers. The department has dedicated experienced and well qualified regular faculty, excellent Biotechnology and Microbiology laboratories with state-of-art-facilities.

DEPARTMENT OF MANAGEMENT

The department of MANAGEMENT is front line department of the College, established in 1996 to provide job/industry oriented professional education. it is dynamic, vibrant and growth oriented. The department has dedicated, experienced and well qualified regular faculty and is supported by distinguished visiting faculty from industry and professionals of consulting and chartered accountancy, etc. It has developed strong linkage with industry which has continuously provided it unstinted support and co-operation.

BACHELOR OF BUSINESS ADMINISTRATION (B.B.A.) B.B.A. : 3 Years (6 Semesters) Higher Secondary (10+2) Certificate examination of any discipline of M.P. Board or its equivalent examination.

DEPARTMENT OF COMMERCE

The department of COMMERCE was established in 1996 to provide job/industry oriented professional through commerce education at undergraduate and post Graduate level. In today’s high-tech society, for successful career in business, Commerce or related professional arena, it is essential to pursue commerce education to gain proficiency in the tools of business organizations, accounting, export/import technicalities, tax law and procedure and information technology. The department has dedicated, experienced and well qualified regular faculty.
