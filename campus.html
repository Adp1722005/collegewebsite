Campus

CAMPUS LOCATION

Located in Indore, Christian Eminent offers a warm and welcoming community infused with urban benefits. Our beautiful campus is literally surrounded by Science and Fine Arts, medical care and research, politics, grassroots activism, and the resounding. We share the neighborhood with more than a dozen other colleges and universities, as well as research centers, restaurants, parks, and other venues.

TECHNOLOGY

Christian Eminent’s beautiful historic campus offers modern learning environments, including faculty and student research facilities, fully equipped Science laboratories, a media centre, and Computer labs. Throughout the campus you will find computer Express Web Stations and many locations with wired and wireless network access.

RESIDENTIAL LIFE

A short walk from the main campus, Christian Eminent’s gated residential campus offers a private refuge in the heart of the city. Nine brick residence halls enclose a grassy quad where students relax or study on sunny days. Dining Hall, Sports Centre, and the campus health center are conveniently located here.

CAREER EDUCATION CENTER

Christian Eminent’s Career Education Center (CEC) provides exceptional career development services and resources for undergraduate and graduate students and alumni.

WELLNESS

Wellness is an important part of life at Christian Eminent. We offer students unlimited access to our award-winning sports centre, healthy eating options in the dining hall, and access to professional health and wellness services, including a campus health center, counseling services, health education, fitness programmes, and campus ministries.
